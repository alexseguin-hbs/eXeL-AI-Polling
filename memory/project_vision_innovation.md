**Core Insight:** The platform mirrors how the human mind works — narrowing from many background processes to focused action. 99→66→33→9→6→3→1. One word. One action. One decision.

**Why:** Collective intelligence requires compression. 1M voices → 9 themes → 6 → 3 → 1 priority. Flower of Life visualization makes this compression visible and intuitive.

**How to apply:**
- Theme hierarchy (99→9→6→3→1) is the core UX metaphor — every design decision should reinforce this narrowing
- "Marble-Grouping" — responses clustered into progressively fewer groups, like marbles sorting into bowls
- Native language programme — crowdsourcing starts local (Austin TX) then aggregates globally in original languages, AI processes in English
- The platform serves multiple products: eXeL AI (kids robot for AI training), MASS-AI (R2D2 industrial inspection + sensor fusion), drone/robotic competitions

**Austin TX Lead:** First pilot city for global crowdsourced innovation. Willing countries follow.
